The data is coming from ATLAS particle detector to find the standard model Higgs boson in pp collisions at s = 13 TeV at the LHC using simulated data. It has a signal file(higgs) and a background file(qcd).The data is measurement of jets that are emitted during proton-proton collisions. It has ['pt', 'eta', 'phi', 'mass', 'ee2', 'ee3', 'd2', 'angularity', 't1', 't2', 't3', 't21', 't32', 'KtDeltaR']14 values that we can explore more.
